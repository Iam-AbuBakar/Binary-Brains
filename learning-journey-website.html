The Student Learning Journey

From registration to your first learning session in 7 simple steps

1 Login / Registration

Your journey starts with a simple sign-up process:

  • Create your account with basic information
  • Verify your email
  • Set up your student profile

Once completed, you'll automatically be directed to the learning assessment phase.

2 Psychometric Test

Discover your unique learning preferences through our comprehensive assessment:

  • Learning modality: Visual / Auditory / Kinesthetic

  • Learning pace: Fast / Moderate / Step-by-step

Note: These results are used exclusively for matching you with compatible educators and are not directly shared with either party.

4 Intellect Badge Test

5 Educator Matching

Our intelligent algorithm finds your ideal learning partners:

  • System filters educators who teach your selected subject
  • Compares your learning style from the psychometric test with educator teaching styles
  • Uses advanced matching algorithms (cosine similarity or weighted scoring) to determine compatibility
  • Presents the top 5 most compatible educators for your consideration

6 Educator Preview & Selection

Review potential educators and make your choice:

  • View detailed educator profiles including bio and teaching methods
  • See your compatibility percentage with each educator
  • Review their teaching style in relation to your learning preferences
  • Your intellect badge is displayed to help educators understand your starting point
  • Select the educator you feel most comfortable with
